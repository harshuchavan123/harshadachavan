Chiken Biryani

ingradient

  • Basmati Rice
  • Oil
  • Garam masale
  • Onion
  • Cumin Seed
  • Ginger Root
  • Chiken
  • Turmeric
  • Salt
  • Ground Cumin
  • Black Paper
  • Lemon Juice

Process

  1. Make the spice blend
  2. Marinate the chicken
  3. Wash the rice and prepare the onions and the potatoes
  4. Cook the chicken
  5. Par-cook the rice
  6. Layer and bake your biryani
  7. Make a quick salad
  8. Finish and serve
